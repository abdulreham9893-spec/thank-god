Our Story

FairyTales is a collection of 10,000 unique, charming, and powerful fairies. The symbolism of a fairy links to the environment, representing the connection between living beings and nature. Known to protect the face of Mother Earth and signify feminine beauty.


FairyTales is founded and created by two female graphic artists looking to pursue their dreams as graphic designers. Further promoting inclusivity and equality through female-oriented artwork that contributes to the scarce female presence in the NFT world. FairyTales strives to make a positive and impactful change in an attempt to reverse the environmental damages caused by today’s society. Commencing fairies brings a diverse concept to enter the world of Non- Fungible Tokens by leading a green community into the space.


Join us in our ever-growing movement into helping the future of our planet. From pollution, deforestation, and climate change, to preserving the lives of endangered species. Our planet needs our help. FairyTales is on a mission to accelerate action, change, and awareness. With the supernatural abilities of our fairies, we are here to make a difference. Mediators between plants, animals, and the various elements of Fairies act as nature.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where Can I View my NFTs?

Once minted, you will connect your opensea account to view your NFTs.

When will the presale and public mint take place?

TBD. Building a strong community behind our project is our main focus. When
the time is right, the dates will be announced via discord and twitter.

How Do I Get on the Whitelist?

Be active and engage with the community. Whitelist spots will be given to
active members of our discord. In addition, whitelist giveaways will take
place in discord and on our twitter page. Follow us on twitter and join our
discord for more information.

How much is the mint sale?

The initial mint price will be set to 0.05 ETH. There will be a 24-hour presale for
whitelisted members, followed by a public mint that will be open until all Fairies
are minted and released onto the blockchain.

What does the future look like for FairyTales NFT?

FairyTales started with just two female designers with a dream. A dream to pursue their careers as graphic designers and truly make a difference in the world for the better. The blockchain gives us a perfect platform to do this. As we build our community and brand, we plan on pursuing valuable partnerships in the space to take FairyTales to the next level in terms of presence and importance. Together, as a community, we will decide the future of this project as we re-invest and find different ways to grow. This is only the beginning!
